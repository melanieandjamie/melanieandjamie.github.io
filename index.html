Transportation
There are 3 airports you can fly into in the NYC area:
1.) LGA is by far the most convenient to the venue (~20 min by car), but it does not have as many direct flight options.
2.) JFK is the second best in terms of convenience and has more flight options. It is 2 trains from the venue or 40 min by car without traffic.
3.) EWR is comparable to JFK by car but more complicated by public transit. It is 4 trains from the venue or 40 min by car without traffic.

There are also many buses and trains that will drop you at convenient transportation hubs in the city.
FAQs
Is there a dress code?
We would love to celebrate with you in whatever you like to wear for special occasions. If that's extremely formal, go for it! If that's more dressy casual, also great! Clothing from any culture is welcome and feel free to wear any color (including white). Festive colors encouraged!
Will we be indoors or outdoors?
All of the wedding events will take place in one air-conditioned building. There is a small side courtyard for those who need to step out for a quick break. There will be no need to walk on grass or soft surfaces so heels are fine. If you are planning to go out in Williamsburg afterward, you may want comfortable walking shoes.
Are kids welcome?
Yes definitely! We will be serving alcohol and we may play music with explicit lyrics, so we respect whatever you feel is best for your kids. If a young child needs a quiet room to nap, we can accommodate this.
Is there parking at the venue?
No. We recommend using a ridshare/taxi or taking the subway. If you really prefer to drive your own car, SpotHero is a great app to find parking deals. Two nearby parking facilities are located at 110 Broadway (~$22) and 75 S 6th St (~$11).
How do I RSVP?
You can RSVP through your Greenvelope invitation which you will receive in a couple months by email.
What does Mango look like and will he be part of the ceremony?
Mango will be celebrating privately with friends of his own species.
