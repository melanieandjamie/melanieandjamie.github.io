FAQs
Is there a dress code?
We would love to celebrate with you in whatever you love to wear. If that's extremely formal, go for it! If that's much more casual, also great! Clothing from any culture is welcome and feel free to wear any color (including white). Festive colors/patterns encouraged!
Will we be indoors or outdoors?
All of the wedding events will take place in one air-conditioned building. There is a small side courtyard for those who need to step out for a quick break. There will be no need to walk on grass or soft surfaces so heels are fine. If you are planning to go out in Williamsburg afterward, you may want comfortable walking shoes.
Are kids welcome?
Yes definitely! We will be serving alcohol and we may play music with explicit lyrics though, so we respect whatever you feel is best for your kids. If a young child needs a quiet room to nap, we can accommodate this.
Is there parking at the venue?
No. You could try to find street parking, although this will likely be slow if you are not familiar with NYC. SpotHero is a great app to find parking deals nearby. Two nearby parking facilities are located at 110 Broadway (~$22) and 75 S 6th St (~$8).
What does Mango look like and will he be part of the ceremony?
Unfortunately, he feels nervous in large groups of humans, so he will be celebrating privately with friends of his own species.
